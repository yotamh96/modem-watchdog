## Gaps

### G1 — Fleet-triple identity preflight exhausts retry budget on cold boot (SC #1)

- **Symptom:** Daemon exits 78/CONFIG ~2s after `systemctl start`, before
  `_production_main` runs. Journal: `unknown fleet triple: failed to
  compute local fleet triple: dms_get_revision failed after 3 attempts`.
- **Phase 05.6 regression?** No. The failure path is the **fleet-triple
  identity preflight** added pre-05.6 and hotfixed in 05.5 with a 3-attempt
  retry. All 3 attempts exhausted on this boot — i.e. either the environment
  is bad or the retry budget is too short for cold boot.
- **Blast radius:** SC #2 / SC #3 / SC #4 / SC #5 are CASCADE-blocked
  (they all require the daemon to be active). SC #6 / SC #7 are CI-runner
  tests, not bench-Jetson-dependent, so they remain runnable independently.
- **Triage steps (operator):**
  1. Confirm modems are powered + visible: `qmicli -p -d /dev/cdc-wdm0 --dms-get-revision` (no daemon) for each of cdc-wdm0..3.
  2. Confirm `ModemManager.service` is `disabled` and not running.
  3. Confirm Zao status — if Zao is running and holds the QMI channels exclusively, the daemon's identity preflight must run before Zao binds, OR the daemon needs a "wait for QMI" preflight delay.
  4. Read `/var/log/qmi-proxy.log` (if any) for the 3 failed attempts.
  5. If modems/Zao are fine: extend the retry budget (Phase 05.5 hotfix was 3 attempts × 0.5s = 1.5s; cold-boot QMI may need 5-10s).
- **Possible follow-up:** Phase 05.7 "fleet-triple cold-boot retry budget" — bump retry count/window OR add a "wait for QMI ready" preflight gate.
- **Verifier override:** SCs #6 + #7 (pytest on aarch64 CI) remain independently testable; do not block them on this gap.
